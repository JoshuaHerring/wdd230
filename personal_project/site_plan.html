Overview

Purpose

Rexburg Hiking is a website designed to show good hiking spots in and around rexburg. There will be the generalized homepage and several subpages like a page sorted by distance, a page to submit reviews on the trails etc

Audience

My target audience is young adults in Rexburg Idaho. The greater part of my audience will be the students of BYU-I because that is the largest group of young adults but it is also for anyone living in the town and passerbyers.
